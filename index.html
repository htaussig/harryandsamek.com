The Attunement Residency

A Week of Being Fully Human Together
September 8-14, 2025 • Portland, Oregon

Welcome to The Attunement Residency!

Picture eight passionate skateboarders flying out to San Francisco, sleeping in Tony Hawk's basement, skating day and night, eating together, joking around —— all for the love of skating.

We're doing the same thing, but our passion is deep conversation and authentic connection.

Who is this for?

Harry and Samek's friends who are already passionate about and skilled in non-agended, open-hearted inquiry.

You're excited to spend a week swapping 4 inquiry conversations a day simply for the joy of doing just that – the way a skateboarder skateboards just because they love to skate.

What We'll Do Together

Our daily activities and rituals

💬

Inquiry Conversations

2-4 hours daily of 1-on-1 conversations, 20 minutes each way, asking questions from an open heart without agenda

🙏

Gratitude Practice

Twice-daily gratitude circles to ground our experience and cultivate an open heart

🍞

Community Meals

Cook, eat, and connect together over shared meals

👟

Movement

Optional group exercise to keep our bodies engaged and energized

⭕

Opening & Closing Circles

Daily group reflection on what we've learned, enjoyed, struggled with, and appreciated
